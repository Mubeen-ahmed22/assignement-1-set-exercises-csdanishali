Nihari

Nihari is a dish made in Pakistan and is a very flavorful dish, made with tender meat alot of differnt spices and a rich spicy gravy. It's often served with naan but some pepople prefer it with rice too.

These are the basic Ingredients used to make the nihari dish, These are the common Ingredients many people use but some people may change it up

  • 1 kg beef or mutton (with bones)
  • 1/2 cup ghee or oil
  • 2 onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup wheat flour (for thickening)
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 green cardamoms
  • 6-8 cups water
  • Fresh coriander and ginger (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving and to get that tangy flavor)

Method

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a heavy-bottomed pot and saut√© sliced onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add meat and sear it until browned.
  4. Add all the spices and mix well.
  5. Pour in water, cover, and simmer for 4-6 hours until the meat is tender.
  6. In a separate pan, roast wheat flour until golden and mix with a little water to make a paste.
  7. Slowly add the wheat flour paste to the Nihari, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  8. Cook for another 30 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander, sliced ginger, and lemon wedges.
  10. Serve hot with naan or rice.
